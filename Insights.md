# Business Insights

## Executive Dashboard

- The organization has **1,470 employees**.
- Overall attrition rate is **16.1%**.
- Average employee salary is **112.96K**.
- Average employee age is **28.99 years**.

---

## Attrition Analysis

### Overtime

Employees working overtime leave more frequently than employees who do not.

### Salary

Employees in the low salary band have the highest attrition.

### Marital Status

Single employees experience higher attrition compared to married and divorced employees.

### Experience

Employees leaving the company stay for an average of **2.43 years**.

### Key Influencers

Power BI Key Influencers identified the following major drivers:

- Younger employees (around 28–29 years)
- Employees with fewer years at the company
- Employees working overtime
- Sales Representatives
- Recruiters
- Low salary employees
- Frequent business travellers

---

## Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Improve retention of new employees.
- Review compensation for lower salary bands.
- Increase employee engagement for high-risk departments.
- Strengthen work-life balance initiatives.
