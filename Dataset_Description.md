# Dataset Description

## Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition using an HR Analytics dataset. The objective is to identify the major factors contributing to employee turnover and present actionable business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

---

## Dataset Tables

### Employee
Contains employee demographic and employment information.

Important columns:
- EmployeeID
- Age
- Gender
- Department
- JobRole
- MaritalStatus
- BusinessTravel
- OverTime
- Salary
- DistanceFromHome (KM)
- YearsAtCompany
- YearsWithCurrManager
- Attrition

---

### PerformanceRating

Contains employee satisfaction and performance metrics.

Important columns:
- EnvironmentSatisfaction
- JobSatisfaction
- RelationshipSatisfaction
- WorkLifeBalance
- ManagerRating
- SelfRating
- TrainingOpportunitiesTaken

---

### EducationLevel

Maps education IDs to education levels.

Examples:
- High School
- Bachelor's
- Master's
- Doctorate

---

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Employees
- Attrition Rate
- Employees Left
- Average Salary
- Average Age
- Average Years Before Leaving
- Employees Leaving with Overtime
